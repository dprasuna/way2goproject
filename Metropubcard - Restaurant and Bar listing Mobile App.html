    Metropubcard – New Way To Dining Out Anywhere Anytime

    It is a membership based Hybrid Mobile App for listing and it will find for you the best Restaurant and Bars nearby you with discounts and special offers. It will you to find the nearest dining out places anytime. You can search by any place or just enter the zip code.

    Metropubcard Mobile App features:

    • Through App one can easily find the nearest Restaurants or Bars according to current location within 50 Miles or you can enter place or zip code.
    • In Mobile App, map navigation system makes it easy for user to track and to locate at desire Restaurant or Bar.
    • Through Mobile App User can create his account on Metropubcard and can avail benefits of membership offers.
    • After getting the membership, Mobile App provides you a barcode, through barcode you can grab all the exciting offers from desired Restaurants or Bars for a fixed time period.
    • The functionality of mobile app also protects user’s membership through barcode. Where this barcode cannot be shared with anyone else or nobody can take its screenshot.
    • Ionic based Mobile app that ensures the speed of Mobile App with user experience like feature access, camera, GPS.
